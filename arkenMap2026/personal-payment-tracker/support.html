Privacy Policy

What the app is for

Personal Payment Tracker helps you log and review payments and budgets on your own device. It is not a bank and does not move money.

Common questions

  • Data location. Your entries are stored locally on your device unless a future update clearly offers optional sync and you choose to use it.
  • Lost phone. If you did not back up the device, local app data may not be recoverable. Use regular iCloud or computer backups if you need recovery options.
  • Wrong totals or categories. Double-check amounts and dates you entered. If something looks like a bug, file an issue with steps to reproduce.
  • Feature requests. Use GitHub Issues; describe how you’d use the feature day to day.

Disclaimer

This app is for personal organization only. It is not financial, tax, or legal advice.
