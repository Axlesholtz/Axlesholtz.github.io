What ArkenMap does

ArkenMap lets you import PDF maps that include georeference information (for example GeoPDF-style measure data), view them full screen, and optionally show your current location on the map when you allow Location access. Your imported maps are stored on your device.

Common issues

  • Location dot missing or wrong. In iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services, ensure Location is on for ArkenMap and set to “While Using the App” (or broader if you choose). On the simulator, set a custom location under Features → Location. GPS accuracy depends on your environment; PDF alignment depends on how the map was georeferenced.
  • Map doesn’t line up with GPS. The app interprets coordinates embedded in the PDF. If the publisher used a different datum or projection than the app expects, you may see a shift. Try re-exporting the PDF with clear CRS metadata from your GIS tool, or open an issue with a minimal example if you think it’s a bug.
  • PDF imports but isn’t “geo”. ArkenMap needs georeferencing metadata in the PDF (not just a picture of a map). Flattened or scanned PDFs without measure/GPTS-style data won’t support the blue-dot overlay.
  • Import fails or file won’t open. Try importing again from the Files app. Very large PDFs may be slow on older devices.
